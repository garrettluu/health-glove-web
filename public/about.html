About Health Glove

Hello! We are Team 13 for IEEE QP Winter 2019!

Health Glove is a wearable that measures one's heart rate and body temperature, and compiles the information onto a web app so that users can access their current heartrate and body temperature in real time. A graph is also included so that users can see trends.

In a world where people are more concerned about their health and wellbeing, access to doctors or to equipment used to monitor health can be hard to get and can be costly, and may be an inconvenience to use during peopleâ€™s daily lives. While there are technologies such as smart watches that can track activity, many people may not want luxury items or may consider them too expensive and most also have to be constantly recharged. Hence, we looked into ways to improve this.

We wanted to build a device for people to monitor their heart rate and their temperature. We decided that we would incorporate the measuring devices into a glove, so that users can measure the above while they are exercising, or during day-to-day activities, and get the data onto a user-friendly website.
